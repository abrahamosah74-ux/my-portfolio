Frequently Asked Questions

Addressing common concerns about working with a developer in Ghana

Is there a language barrier?

Not at all. English is Ghana's official language, and I'm fluent in both technical and business English. All communication, documentation, and code comments are in clear, professional English.

How do time zones work?

I'm in Ghana (GMT). For US clients, I'm typically 4-8 hours ahead, which means I can make progress overnight. For UK/European clients, we're in the same or similar time zones. I adjust my schedule to ensure overlap for meetings and real-time communication.

What about internet reliability in Ghana?

I have dual fiber optic internet connections with automatic failover to mobile 4G/5G backup. My uptime monitoring shows 99.8% availability. Major cities in Ghana have reliable, high-speed internet comparable to many Western countries.

How do payments work?

I use secure international payment platforms: PayPal, Wise (TransferWise), and direct bank transfers. We agree on milestones with clear deliverables. I've never had payment issues with international clients.

Can you handle Ghana-specific requirements?

Absolutely! This is one of my unique advantages. I can integrate Mobile Money payments, optimize for local hosting, implement Ghana-specific SEO, and ensure cultural appropriateness for Ghanaian audiences.
